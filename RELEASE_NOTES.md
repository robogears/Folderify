# What's new in v0.1.11

## Up Next — a real queue

- **Right-click any track → "Add to queue" or "Play next".** Queued songs play before
  your current playlist resumes — just like Spotify's queue — without losing your place.
- A new **Up Next** panel (the queue button in the player bar, with a count badge) shows
  what's coming, with per-track play / remove and a **Clear** button.

## Queue together

- When you're **listening together**, both of you can queue songs and **see each other's
  Up Next** ("Queued by \<name\>"). Whatever's queued is the next song that plays — on
  **both** Macs. (Listen Together is still experimental and LAN-only.)

# Install / update

- **Already on v0.1.2 or later?** Just click the in-app **Update** button (top bar or
  Settings → Updates) — it downloads and self-installs. No DMG needed.
- **Fresh install (macOS, Apple Silicon):** download `Folderify-0.1.11-arm64.dmg`, open
  it, and drag Folderify to Applications.

On a fresh install, macOS Gatekeeper holds the app back on first launch (it isn't
notarized). On macOS 15+: try to open it, then go to **System Settings → Privacy &
Security → Open Anyway** — once only.

Your settings, metadata cache, and thumbnails live in `~/Library/Application Support/Folderify/`.

## Requirements

- macOS 11 (Big Sur) or later, on an Apple Silicon Mac.
- No account, API key, or subscription.
